Hello,

I'M BEN HOPKINS

I’m the founder and perfumer at Lōtos Fragrances. My journey is fuelled by a passion for creating, and an urge to savour the process. My mission goes beyond fragrances; it's about helping people discover comfort and tranquility through the captivating world of scents.

Everything I make is a love letter to the beauty of nature. I strive to capture the essence of plants, the very spirit of the air, and our profound connection with the natural world.

OUR PHILOSOPHY

At the core of our philosophy is a dedication to weaving captivating stories through scent compositions.

Grounded in Alberta, Lōtos Fragrances draws inspiration from the region's rugged landscapes and pristine air. This connection to our place in nature infuses our scents with a distinct character, capturing the essence of the Alberta wilderness in every bottle. Our fragrances are meticulously crafted using high-quality natural ingredients. These scents invite individuals to immerse themselves in sensory experiences that celebrate our connection to nature.
Each note and blend is thoughtfully chosen to create immersive aromatic landscapes that resonate with the natural world. We embrace the richness of quality, natural ingredients, steering clear of industry trends and the pull of mass production.

We strive to create a temporary escape into a world of sensory bliss, where time slows, and the complexities of everyday life momentarily fade away. Lōtos Fragrances is about celebrating simple pleasures, embracing our connection to the environment, and crafting aromatic landscapes that stand the test of time.
